**Critical formatting rule: `scope:` MUST be added inside the YAML
frontmatter block, between the two `---` lines — never as a new section

same block bounded by `---` above and `---` below):

```
---
title: Example ticket
status: todo
priority: 3
scope:
  - src/components/example.tsx
  - server.cjs
created: 2026-01-01
---
```
